Our Solutions

Simplified solutions
for every municipality

Each IMS app is designed to be easy to understand, cost-effective, and ready to use from day one. Pick the solution that fits your municipality — whether that's one app or all of them.

Permits 🗺️

IMS-Planning — Development Permits

Track your applications to know the status of each project. Use the mapping tool to see how developments will affect residents in other areas.

Permits · Inspections 🏗️

Building Permits & Site Inspections

Convert those old spreadsheets into a database and easily manage and track building permits. Includes status tracking and statistics. Also send copies of your inspection reports directly to the owner and contractor. Works with contracted service providers as well.

Fire & Life Safety 🔥

Fire Inspections

Why make fire inspections complicated? Use the pre-developed checklist to ensure you don't miss anything while on site. Pre-planning tools and owner-side deficiency reporting make follow-up simple.

Enforcement 📋

Site Reporter — Orders & Site Violations

Need to record issues on site for a possible order or enforcement at a later date? Site Reporter allows you to record notes, deficiencies and smart images immediately.

Communications 📢

Mass Notification System

Want to communicate with a number of people at the same time and record it? Mass Notification allows you to ensure all of your staff, council or citizens are aware at the same time with the same information.

Asset Management 🌾

Rural Municipal Asset Management

Track and manage rural municipal assets including roads, culverts, bridges, and infrastructure. Condition recording, maintenance history, and reports that satisfy provincial asset management requirements.

Asset Management 🏙️

Urban Municipal Asset Management

Track and manage urban municipal infrastructure including water, sewer, sidewalks, parks, and street lights through their full lifecycle — from installation through maintenance to replacement.

Maintenance 🏢

Building Maintenance & Recording

Keep track of what repairs you completed. Take pictures of damage and record repairs while they are being completed. Build a complete maintenance history for every municipal building.

Maintenance 🚜

Equipment Maintenance & Recording

Pay attention to all the little things. Machine wear, break downs, replacements and more. Take pictures for documentation and warranty. Keep a full maintenance record for every piece of equipment.

Building Codes 📖

Codes Toolbox

A curated reference engine for building code interpretations, CCMC reports, and appeal decisions. Designed for building officials, inspectors, and designers who need fast, reliable code answers.
