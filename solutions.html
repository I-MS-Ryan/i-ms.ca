Our Applications

Every app your
municipality needs

A growing suite of purpose-built apps. Each one works standalone or connects through a shared property record — no duplicate data entry, no disconnected systems.

Land & Development
Permits 🗺️

IMS-Planning — Development Permits

Track applications from submission to approval. GIS map shows all active applications across your municipality. Approvals carry property data directly into building permits — no re-entry required.

Permits · Inspections 🏗️

IMS-Build — Building Permits & Inspections

Replace spreadsheets with a proper permit database. Track all inspection stages to NBC minimums. Mobile inspection app captures photos with GPS timestamps and emails reports to owners and contractors automatically.

Fire & Life Safety
Fire & Life Safety 🔥

IMS-Fire — Fire Inspection & Pre-Planning

Occupancy-based inspection checklists (Group A–F per NBC/SK Fire Code). Pre-incident plans with construction type, access points, utility shutoffs, suppression systems, and hydrant locations — available offline for responding units. Owner-side deficiency reporting via a simple web link, no app install needed. GIS dashboard shows your full inspection program status at a glance.

Compliance & Enforcement
Enforcement 📋

IMS-Site — Orders & Violations

Record site deficiencies on the spot with GPS-tagged, timestamped photos. Generate formal notices and track compliance deadlines. AI-assisted order language drafted from your field notes. Full history per property.

Bylaw 📜

IMS-Bylaw — Bylaw & Enforcement Management

Manage public complaints through investigation to formal resolution. Generate notices, track hearings, and maintain a complete defensible file for every enforcement action. Links directly to Site Reporter field records.

Business & Licensing
Licensing 🪪

IMS-Licence — Business Licensing

Issue and renew licences without paper forms. Online applications, automated renewal notices, and automatic flags when a business type requires a fire inspection before the licence can be issued.

Coming Soon
Asset & Infrastructure Management
Asset Management 🌾

IMS-Asset Rural — Rural Asset Management

Roads, culverts, bridges, and rural infrastructure tracked on a GIS map. Condition recording with photos, maintenance history, and capital replacement reports that satisfy provincial asset management requirements.

Asset Management 🏙️

IMS-Asset Urban — Urban Asset Management

Water mains, sewer, sidewalks, parks, and street lights tracked through their full lifecycle — installation, maintenance, and replacement — with GIS map and detailed history logs.

Maintenance 🏢

IMS-Maintain — Buildings & Equipment

Log repairs, track parts and warranty claims, and set scheduled maintenance reminders. QR codes on assets link to their full history — scan on-site, update instantly.

Infrastructure Inspections
Infrastructure 🛣️

IMS-Road — Road & Infrastructure Inspections

GPS-tracked mobile road condition assessments. Record surface conditions, drainage issues, and deficiencies. AI-assisted pavement distress classification from field photos feeds directly into asset management priorities.

Communications & Mapping
Communications 📢

IMS-Notify — Mass Notification

Send one message to staff, council, or citizens all at once. GIS-based targeting lets you notify everyone in a selected area. Full delivery records for every message sent.

GIS · Mapping 📍

IMS-Map — GIS Mapping Portal

One shared map layer across all IMS apps. Permits, assets, inspections, and violations plotted and searchable in one view. No GIS expertise required.
