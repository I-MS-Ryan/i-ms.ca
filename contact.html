Common Questions

Before you reach out

Do all the apps need to be purchased together?

No — each IMS app works as a standalone product. You can start with the one that solves your most pressing problem and add others over time. They're designed to connect when you're ready.

Can IMS work with our contracted inspectors?

Yes. IMS is designed for multi-jurisdictional use. Whether your inspectors are in-house, contracted, or shared through an intermunicipal agreement, the system handles all of it.

Is IMS compliant with the National Building Code?

Yes. All permitting and inspection workflows are built around NBC requirements and Saskatchewan provincial amendments, and are updated as codes are revised.

How long does setup take?

Most apps are designed to be up and running the same day. There's no lengthy implementation process — your staff shouldn't need weeks of training to manage a permit or run an inspection checklist.

Is our data secure?

All IMS apps use encrypted connections and are built on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure. Your municipal records are stored securely and are only accessible by your authorized users.

What if we need something custom?

Reach out and describe what you need. We work directly with municipalities to understand unique requirements, and some custom configurations are available depending on scope.
