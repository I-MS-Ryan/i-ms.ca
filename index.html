The Problem We Solve

Municipalities deserve
better tools

The expectations placed on municipalities keep growing — but the tools available are either too expensive, too complicated, or built for someone else.

📈

Unrealistic Expectations

Reporting, record keeping, communication, documentation, asset management, GIS — municipalities are expected to manage all of it with limited staff and even more limited budgets.

💸

Costly & Complex Solutions

Enterprise software vendors charge tens to hundreds of thousands of dollars — then add extensive training requirements and ongoing maintenance costs that many smaller municipalities simply can't sustain.

✅

IMS Simplified Solutions

Our goal is straightforward: practical, cost-effective apps that any municipality can pick up and use. From the smallest rural RM to a mid-size city — everyone deserves tools that actually work for them.

Our Applications

Everything your municipality needs,
in one ecosystem

A growing suite of purpose-built apps. Each one is designed to be standalone or work together as a complete municipal platform.

Permits 🗺️

IMS-Planning — Development Permits

Track all development applications from submission to approval. Use the integrated mapping tool to visualize how new developments affect surrounding properties and land use.

Permits · Inspections 🏗️

Building Permits & Site Inspections

Replace spreadsheets with a proper database. Track permits, statuses, and statistics. Send inspection reports directly to owners and contractors. Fully compatible with contracted service providers.

Inspections 🔥

Fire Inspections

Pre-built fire inspection checklists so nothing gets missed on site. Record findings, attach photos, and generate reports — all from a mobile device.

Enforcement 📋

Site Reporter — Orders & Violations

Record site issues, deficiencies, and notes on the spot. Capture smart, timestamped and geotagged images for orders and enforcement action — all documented and ready when you need it.

Communications 📢

Mass Notification System

Communicate with staff, council, or citizens all at once with one message. Full delivery records ensure everyone received the same information at the same time.

Asset Management 🌾

Rural Municipal Asset Management

Designed for rural municipalities and RMs. Track roads, infrastructure, and assets across large geographic areas. Simple enough for small offices, powerful enough to satisfy reporting obligations.

Asset Management 🏙️

Urban Municipal Asset Management

For towns and cities managing complex networks of infrastructure. Track assets through their full lifecycle — from installation to maintenance to replacement — with detailed history logs.

Maintenance 🏢

Building Maintenance & Recording

Keep a running log of all municipal building repairs. Record damage with photos and track repairs as they happen. Know your maintenance history at a glance.

Maintenance 🚜

Equipment Maintenance & Recording

Track machine wear, breakdowns, and replacements. Photo documentation for warranty claims. Stay ahead of failures with a complete maintenance history for every piece of equipment.

GIS · Mapping 📍

GIS Mapping Portal

A shared, up-to-date map layer for your municipality. Plot permits, assets, inspections, and violations on one geographic view. Built for staff use — no GIS expertise required.
