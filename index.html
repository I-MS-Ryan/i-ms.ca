About Us

Why IMS exists

📈

Unrealistic Expectations

In today's world, the expectations placed on municipalities is never ending. Whether its reporting, record keeping, communication, documentation, asset management, GIS and more; there appears to be an unattainable expectation for municipalities to meet.

💸

Expensive / Complicated Solutions

There are many solutions for municipalities to use to combat this unrealistic expectation. However, these current solutions come with some heavy costs, most of the time ranging from tens of thousands to hundreds of thousands of dollars. Additionally, these options typically require many hours of training and maintenance to keep the solutions or software usable.

✅

IMS Simplified Solutions

We do not have all of the answers, however our goal is to provide easy-to-understand, cost-effective solutions that allow municipalities to complete their tasks. We have created these solutions to allow even the smallest municipalities to utilize GIS systems and on-site information, allowing more efficient recording and use of time.
