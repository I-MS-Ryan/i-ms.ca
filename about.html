About IMS

Built by people who
understand the work

IMS was founded on a straightforward premise: Canadian municipalities deserve practical, affordable software — built around how their offices actually operate, not around what enterprise clients wanted a decade ago.

"Dedicated to the
success of municipalities"

That isn't a marketing line — it's the filter every product decision runs through. If it doesn't make a municipal office's work simpler, more accurate, or more defensible, it doesn't ship.

Our Story

The problem was clear. The solution needed to be built.

After years working directly in building code administration, permitting, and inspections across Saskatchewan, it became clear that the tools available to municipalities fell into two categories: paper-based processes held together with spreadsheets, or expensive enterprise systems that required months of implementation and ongoing vendor dependence.

Neither option served the reality of most Canadian municipalities — particularly the smaller cities, towns, villages, and rural municipalities that form the backbone of the country but are routinely underserved by software vendors who design for the largest clients first.

IMS was founded to close that gap. Starting with the inspection and permitting workflows that form the foundation of building safety and municipal compliance, and expanding into a full ecosystem of connected apps — each one simple enough to use on day one, and built around the minimum industry-standard requirements for each scope of work.

"The tools should work for the municipality — not the other way around."

Our Principles

What guides every decision we make

🎯

Simplicity First

Every feature earns its place. If it doesn't complete an essential task more easily than before, it doesn't ship. Complexity is the enemy of adoption.

⚖️

Minimum Industry Standard

Every app is built to complete the minimum required tasks for its scope — the same standard a municipal inspector, fire chief, or administrator would be held to professionally.

🔗

Connected by Design

Apps that share data are more useful than apps that stand alone. The shared property record is the backbone of the IMS ecosystem — no double entry, no disconnected records.

💰

Accessible to All

A village of 400 people deserves the same quality tools as a city of 40,000. Pricing and complexity should never be a barrier to good municipal governance.

📱

Field-Ready

Built for the people doing the work — not the people watching it on a dashboard. Mobile-first, offline-capable, and designed for real field conditions.

🛡️

Defensible Records

Every record created in IMS is timestamped, geotagged, and traceable. When decisions are challenged, your documentation is ready.
